Module Updates and Quality Assurance

Sundays Energy is dedicated to ensuring that your website is reliable, bug-free and compliant with current web standards.

However, given the open-source nature of the platform, there are some aspects which may be beyond our control. This applies specifically to certain non-core modules contributed by the Drupal community, and sites that are hosted on servers other than our own.

In the cases above, there may occasionally be issues requiring us to update the software your website is running on.
