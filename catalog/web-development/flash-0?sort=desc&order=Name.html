Flash

Adobe Flash, commonly known as Flash, refers to the Adobe Flash Player used on the Web. Introduced in 1996, Flash technology has become a popular method for adding animation and interactivity to web pages. Flash is often used to create animation, advertisements, various web-page components, to integrate video into web pages, and more recently, to develop rich Internet applications.
