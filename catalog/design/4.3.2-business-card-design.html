4.3.2 Business Card Design

in
  • Design
4.3.2

• Our creative team will develop layouts and copy for business cards.
• In an initial meeting, Client and Developer determine the goal for the marketing material, in line with Client’s mission and vision.
• Developer will create a sketch of design ideas to be presented to the Client including vision and impact of the marketing material.
• Design must be signed off by the Client to move forward for development of materials.
• Final approval will be requested by Client before material is created.
• Final material is property of the Clients. All other proofs and ideas will be property of Developer. If Client wishes to use other proofs, an additional fee will be applied.
