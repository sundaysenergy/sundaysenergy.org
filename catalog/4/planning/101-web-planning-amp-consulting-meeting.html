1.0.1 Web Planning & Consulting Meeting

in

1.0.1

Planning meetings include a general overview and assessment of the site’s core functionality. May include:
* Comprehensive planning of the website’s functionality
* Selection of key features and modules
* Budgeting and Strategy for website
* Confirm audiences and general marketing approach
* General Strategy for Web presence within business plan: operations and marketing
* Highest-level consultant presence.
* Additional Marketing & Renewable Energy Consulting as requested
Billed by the hour. Often two or more SE staff are present in these meetings.
