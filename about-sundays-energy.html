About Sundays Energy

Sundays Energy endeavors to bring together individuals and organizations that feel passionately about doing what they can to help the earth and the creatures living on it. Sundays Energy helps enable others to become part of this undertaking in their own unique way, to realize the personal and community-wide benefits from working and living sustainably.

One part of this is Sundays Energy's role as a green business incubator. SE provides a variety of services to aspiring environmentally friendly entrepreneurs. By joining with Sundays Energy, they will be immediately connected to a support system that can help with everything from business proposals to branding, and taking care of the many logistics necessary for a successful business to operate.
