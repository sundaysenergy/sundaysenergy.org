Sundays Energy Mission

Sundays Energy is a local, employee-owned company dedicated to advancing environmental sustainability by serving as a comprehensive resource for everything green. Experienced in creativity, conservation, ecology, and renewable energy.

Our Mission:

  • Unite communities, farmers, businesses and investors in renewable energy initiatives through an integrated, diversified, member-owned company.
  • Reduce regional and world dependence on unstable and polluting energy sources by facilitating conservation and use of “green” technologies such as biodiesel, solar, and wind.
  • Build a profitable enterprise that offers financial security to its members and those employed.
